About

Welcome to UniMuse! Have you ever found yourself opening up Spotify for one song, then YouTube for a live version or remix of that song? Well, you've come to the right place! UniMuse is a non-proprietary web application that consolidates music services, such as Spotify, Mixcloud, and YouTube, providing you an easy-to-use, all-inclusive platform for all of your music needs.
